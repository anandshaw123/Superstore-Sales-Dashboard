## **Business Questions**

- Which region and segment contribute the most to total sales and profit?

- How do sales and profit trends vary across months and years (2019 vs 2020)?

- Which payment mode and ship mode are preferred by customers?

- Which product categories and sub-categories generate the highest sales?

- What are the top-performing states in terms of profit and sales?

- Are there any underperforming segments or regions that require strategic attention?




## **Business Objectives**

- To analyze sales performance across regions, segments, and categories.

- To identify key drivers of profitability (payment, ship mode, and region).

- To evaluate year-over-year growth trends in sales and profit.

- To optimize business strategy by focusing on high-performing areas and addressing low-performing ones.

- To support decision-making with clear visual insights for marketing, logistics, and sales planning.




## **Tools Used**

- Microsoft Power BI for dashboard visualization.
